स्वयमसिद्ध HACKATHON 20-20

Rules And Steps

  • Step 1-First Register on website
  • Step 2-Choose your sector from website
  • Step 3-Put your ideas and description in detail
  • Step 4-First of all we will have to select best three ideas from each sector
  • Step 5-Selected participants have to give the detail explanation and solution of their ideas with PPT
  • Step 6-Then selection of best 10 solution from 30 will be done
  • Step 7-Working process on that ideas will be started after the reopening of college
  • Step 8-Then we will to apply that the proposal and we will upload that project to UBA (govt) for providing funds for that project.(projects from our college)

Selection Criteria

Idea Selection process will be depend on following points

  • Creativity--"wow" factor to solve problem
  • Usability-- usefulness of solution
  • Technicality--technical use of the solution
  • Scalability--potential for future upgrade to improve the solution

*Terms and Conditions*

  • The technical project will depends on covid 19 issue/problem or problems after lockdown
  • Problem should be self identified and have to give technical solution
  • Create ur own project ,do not copy other's project
  • participants will have to present PPT on zoom app after selection of project
  • After selection of best idea the process of working will be started after lockdown when college will reopen
  • All participants will get certificates
