स्वयंसिद्ध HACKATHON 2020

Rules

  • 1. The ideas should be from the mentioned sectors only. The sectors are as follows:
    •      a. Agriculture and Ruler Development
         b. Water Management
         c. Energy Resources
         d. Artisan, Industries and livelihood
         e. Healthcare and Biochemical Devices
         f. Basic Education and training
         g. Infrastructure
         h. Women Empowerment
  • Problem should be self identified and should give technical solution.
  • In case of copy or duplicate ideas, the idea as well as the team will be disqualified.
  • Maximum 5 members are allowed in a single team.
  • Multiple ideas / solutions to a problem(s) can be submitted by a single team .
  • Make sure the registered team name is unique.
  • Shortlisted participants will have to present the idea on the platform provided by the organizers.
  • The best shortlisted project idea will be asked for project implementation, after colleges reopen.
  • Participation E-Certificate will be awarded to all the participants.
  • No registration fees.
  • The copyrights of the implemented projects will remain with the respective team although; it can be implemented by the organizing institute (AISSMS COE) for any purpose.
  • There will be open defense but the final evalution will be prepared by evaluators
  • All rights reserved with the institute

Rules Regarding Presentation

  • Participants selected in the Round 1 will have to give a brief presentation on the idea.
  • Selected participants will have to present the idea on given platform.
  • The presentation has to be based on the submitted idea in Round 1 only. Otherwise, the team will be disqualified
  • All the participating teams have to login for presentation of their ideas 10 minutes before the given time to make sure of all the technicalities during the presentation.
  • The time limit for a presentation is 10 minutes. After 12 minutes, the presenter will be given a stop alarm.
  • Before proceeding with the presentation, all the team members are requested to switch on their video and if required they can unmute themselves.
  • Other teams should switch off their microphone and video when 1 team is giving their presentation.
  • Time limitation should be strictly followed.
  • Teams should not share their information such as city, state, name of institute at the time of presentation.
  • Only jury members will ask questions to the presenter.
  • Participants may leave the meeting once they have finished with their presentation.
  • E-certificates will only be provided to those participants who have completed their presentation.
  • Submission of the idea should strictly followed according to given template
  • Only 3 slides are allowed in the presentation,there are no limitations for number of words
  • At the time of presentation, all the participant should follows cyber ethics
